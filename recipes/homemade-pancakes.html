Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, milk, egg, butter, sugar, baking powder, and salt together.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour batter onto griddle, and cook until lightly browned, about 2 minutes.
  3. Flip pancakes; cook until browned on both sides, about 2 minutes more.
  4. Serve immediately with butter and maple syrup, if desired.
