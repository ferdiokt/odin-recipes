Ingredients:

  • 5 teaspoons olive oil
  • 4 shallots, diced
  • 1 large onion, cut into thin strips
  • 1 pound bacon, cut into strips
  • 1 clove garlic, shopped
  • 1 package Fettucini pasta
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 3/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add shallots, onion, bacon, and garlic. Cook, stirring occasionally, until bacon is golden brown, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add pasta and egg yolks. Cook, stirring occasionally, until pasta is cooked through, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add cream, Parmesan cheese, and salt and pepper to taste. Cook, stirring occasionally, until cheese is melted, about 1 minute.
  4. Serve immediately with additional Parmesan cheese for sprinkling.
